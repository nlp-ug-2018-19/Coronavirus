What is it all about?

Painting is a form of visual art in which a person creates a two-dimensional image with the use of paint and usually brushes. An artist can choose between a variety of mediums, such as tempera, fresco, oil, acrylic, watercolour or other water-based paints, ink, gouache, encaustic, or casein. Moreover, there are many forms one can choose when painting: mural, easel, panel, miniature, manuscript illumination, scroll, screen or fan, panorama, or any of a variety of modern forms, including digital painting.
